Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

We currently service all major tourist destinations including Orlando, Miami, Las Vegas, San Diego, and New York City. Our delivery radius typically extends 25 miles from city centers, but we can often accommodate special requests. Please check our location finder or contact us for specific address verification.

We're continuously expanding our service areas, so if you don't see your location listed, please reach out as we may still be able to assist you.

We offer convenient delivery and pickup at your hotel, resort, vacation rental, cruise port, or private residence. At the time of booking, you'll select your delivery location and preferred time window. Our team will deliver the equipment, provide a brief orientation on its use, and pick it up at the end of your rental period.

Same-day delivery is available for orders placed before 2pm in most locations. For cruise ship rentals, we coordinate directly with port authorities to ensure seamless delivery to your ship.

Rental rates vary by equipment type, duration, and location. We offer competitive pricing with significant discounts for longer rental periods. All prices include delivery, pickup, and basic insurance coverage.

As a general guideline for our most popular items:

  • Standard wheelchairs: $15-25 per day
  • Electric scooters: $25-45 per day
  • Knee walkers: $20-35 per day
  • Medical equipment: $15-40 per day
  • Accessories: $5-15 per day

We offer significant discounts for weekly and monthly rentals. Please visit our rental page or contact us for specific pricing based on your needs and location.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), debit cards, and PayPal. We also accept HSA/FSA cards for qualifying medical equipment rentals. Payment is required at the time of booking to secure your reservation.

For corporate accounts and travel agencies, we offer net-30 payment terms upon approval. Contact our business services team for more information.

You may cancel your reservation up to 24 hours before your scheduled delivery time for a full refund. Cancellations within 24 hours of delivery will be charged a $25 late cancellation fee. No-shows or same-day cancellations will be charged 50% of the rental total.

In cases of medical emergencies or flight cancellations, please contact us and we'll work with you to accommodate your situation, including rescheduling your rental at no additional charge when possible.
